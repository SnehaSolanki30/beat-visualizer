Beat Detection Algorithm

This implementation uses an energy-based beat detection algorithm that analyzes audio in real-time:

  1. Energy Calculation: Computes the instantaneous energy of the audio signal by summing the squares of frequency bin magnitudes from the FFT analysis.
  2. Moving Average: Maintains a rolling average of recent energy values to establish a baseline energy level.
  3. Peak Detection: Identifies beats when the current energy exceeds the moving average by a threshold factor (sensitivity), with temporal constraints to prevent false positives from rapid fluctuations.
  4. BPM Estimation: Calculates beats per minute by tracking the time intervals between detected beats and averaging over recent detections.
